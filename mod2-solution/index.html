The KETO Diet

80% Fats

Fats include Coconut oil, olive oil, grass fed butter, avocado, nuts and seeds,high-fat dairy.

15% Protein

Protein includes eggs, chicken, beef, pork, fish.

5% Carbs

Carbs include above ground veggies (broccoli, cauliflower, lettuce, zucchini). Leafy greens (spinach and kale). Low glycemic impact berries (rasberries, blackberries, etc.)
